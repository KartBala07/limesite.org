Support Us

Citrus Circuits is funded through the Davis Robotics Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Whether through donations, sponsorship, or volunteer hours, every bit of support keeps the team running.

Davis Robotics Foundation

The Davis Robotics Foundation (DRF) is a nonprofit corporation, incorporated in California on October 10, 2016, organized to support STEM activities for K–12 students with a focus on robotics. DRF funds the operation of FRC Team 1678, the Davis Youth Robotics program, and associated competition, outreach, and education programs.

Donations to the Foundation are tax-deductible. You can also support DRF through the Big Day of Giving campaign each year.

Nik Murray Memorial Sponsorship

Established in memory of longtime mentor Nik Murray, this sponsorship fund honors his impact on the team by supporting the same mentorship and technical training programs he helped build. The 2024 robot, "Nik," was named in his memory.

Parent Committee

Our Parent Committee coordinates logistics for build season and competitions — meals, travel, fundraising events, and chaperoning — and is always looking for new parent volunteers.

Reimbursement

Mentors and parents who incur approved team expenses (parts, travel, supplies) can submit a reimbursement request to the Davis Robotics Foundation treasurer. Contact us for the current reimbursement form.

Volunteer Opportunities

We need mentors, judges, scouts, drivers, and event-day volunteers throughout the season — technical background not required. Reach out via the Contact page if you'd like to help.
