Our Robots

A new robot every season, almost entirely designed and machined in-house — and almost always with a citrus pun. Drop the real photos into the pictures/ folder and each card fills in automatically.

2025
'25

SubLime

REEFSCAPE

A three-time regional winner that intook both coral and algae and scored on all four reef levels with an elevator and end effector. SubLime climbed a deep cage with ease and scored in both the processor and net.

2024
'24

Nik

CRESCENDO

Named in memory of mentor Nik Murray. Roller mechanisms picked up notes and shot them into the speaker and amp; a hook let Nik hang on the chain in the final seconds while scoring a note in the trap.

2023
'23

Tangerine Tumbler

CHARGED UP

A rolling intake collected cones and cubes and placed them precisely. With the help of LED rods, it could dock quickly — finishing the balance in the final seconds of a match.

2022
'22

Margie

RAPID REACT

Named after our shop dog. Margie stored cargo, used a color sensor to tell alliance colors apart, shot into the high goal, and climbed the traversal rung in about 10 seconds.

2021
'21

Clemen-twins

FRC AT HOME

A tank bot and a swerve bot built for the At-Home skills and auto-path challenges — posting some of the fastest times in the world during the COVID-adjusted season.

2020
'20

Emperor Pulp-atine

INFINITE RECHARGE

Built for the Lucasfilm-partnered game, it collected and stored power cells and climbed the generator switch alongside another robot while staying level.

2019
'19

Buzz Lime-year

DEEP SPACE

Handled cargo and hatch panels across three levels, climbed to the highest platform, and could lift two other robots with it.

2018
'18

Lemon Zest

POWER UP

Picked up milk-crate-sized cubes for the scale and switch, then climbed a rung with an 80-inch hook — lifting a second robot on a platform to "beat the boss."

2017
'17

Pomelo Pummeler

STEAMWORKS

Shot fuel into the high boiler, delivered gears to the airship, and climbed the rope to the airship in the final 30 seconds.

2016
'16

Adrian

STRONGHOLD

A three-time regional winner with nine air tanks that shot balls ~11 feet at 10 mph, crossing every field defense from the low bar to the portcullis.

2015
'15

Lemon Drop

RECYCLE RUSH

Part of the winning alliance at the 2015 FIRST World Championship.

2014
'14

Beca

AERIAL ASSAULT

Two intakes and a high-powered catapult sent balls over the truss into the high goal. Beca played midfield and adapted quickly between strategies.

2013
'13

E-Lime-o-Nator

ULTIMATE ASCENT

Intook Frisbee discs from the ground, shot them into the high goal, and hung from the pyramid for 10 bonus points at the buzzer.

2012
'12

The Juicer

REBOUND RUMBLE

Named after a 72-tooth-sprocket incident, it shot foam basketballs into 96-inch hoops and balanced on the teeter-totter bridges.

2011
'11

Optimus Lime

LOGO MOTION

Our first regional-winning robot — capable of scoring two full logos, an ubertube, and a minibot.

2010
'10

Orange Crush

BREAKAWAY

Shot soccer balls into goals and deployed an arm in the endgame to pull itself up onto a tower.

2009
'09

Lemon Squeeze

Lunacy

It was capable of both picking up stray “moon rocks” from the ground and receiving moon rocks thrown by the payload specialist. The moon rocks were stored in the hopper, with a maximum capacity of 30 balls. When ready to score, a conveyor belt system carried the balls up and out of the robot, shooting them at point-blank range into our opponent's trailer.
