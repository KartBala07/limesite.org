The 2026 Robot

Limestone, our 2026 competition robot, is a two-time district-winning and Milstein Division champion machine built for the REBUILT game.

LIMESTONE
Overview

Limestone

Limestone is Citrus Circuits' 2026 competition robot, built for the REBUILT game. The robot was a two-time district winner and a Milstein Division champion at the FIRST Championship, qualifying for the Einstein Field, and picked up multiple Autonomous Awards along the way.

Designed and machined almost entirely in-house by our design, fabrication, electrical, and software subteams, Limestone carries forward the design language of SubLime (2025) while adapting it to REBUILT's scoring elements.

Scoring

INTAKE
Intake

Floor Pickup & Scoring

Limestone's intake picks game pieces off the floor and feeds them into the robot for scoring, using a set of floor rollers to control and direct pieces into the ball tunnel for a smooth, consistent handoff.

ROLLERS

Ball Tunnel / Shooter

SHOOTER
Scoring

Ball Tunnel & Shooter

Game pieces travel through a dedicated ball tunnel that feeds the shooter, which fires pieces at the scoring target. The shooter and tunnel were tuned together so pieces are delivered consistently regardless of where they enter the robot.

Drivetrain

DRIVE
Mobility

Drivetrain

Limestone runs a swerve drivetrain built in-house, giving the robot full holonomic control on the field for fast, precise positioning during both scoring and defense.

Hopper

A hopper sits above the intake and ball tunnel, storing game pieces collected by the robot and metering them into the shooting path so the drive team can score multiple pieces without re-intaking between shots.

Operating Interface

The operator interface gives the drive team direct control over intake, hopper indexing, and shooting, with feedback built in so operators can confirm a piece is loaded and ready before firing.

Electrical

The electrical subteam wired and maintained Limestone's power distribution, motor controllers, and sensors, keeping the robot's systems reliable across a full competition season of practice and matches.
