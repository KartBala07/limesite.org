Outreach & Service

Citrus Circuits exports its expertise by starting and mentoring DYR and FRC teams, hosting Diversity in STEM events, running workshops, and showing up at the Davis Farmers Market every week.

Citrus Service

Founded in 2017, Citrus Service is our outreach program dedicated to aiding other robotics teams in their competitive pursuits — from spare parts and machining favors to scouting help and strategy support at competitions.

Davis Youth Robotics (DYR)

Davis Youth Robotics is the K-12 robotics pipeline that Citrus Circuits founded and continues to mentor, feeding students into FLL, FTC, and eventually FRC teams across Davis.

About DYR RoboCamps — week-long summer camps introducing robotics fundamentals.
DYR RoboCamps Registration — sign-ups open each spring for the following summer.
DYR RoboCamps Forms — waivers, medical forms, and camper info sheets.
DYR RoboCamps Resources — curriculum and lesson plans for camp counselors.
DYR League — a youth competition league for younger robotics teams in the district.
DYR School Programs — in-classroom robotics units taught at DJUSD elementary and middle schools.
