Capital City Classic

Citrus Circuits' own off-season FRC event, bringing teams from across the region together for a day of competition, scrimmages, and technical workshops led by our students and mentors.

The Capital City Classic is an off-season event hosted and run by Citrus Circuits in Davis, California. Teams from across Northern California bring their robots out of storage for a low-stakes day of matches, giving rookies and veterans alike a chance to test new mechanisms, train new drive teams, and scout the competition ahead of the next build season.

Alongside the on-field matches, the event features a set of technical workshops led by Citrus Circuits students and mentors, covering topics like CAD, programming, and scouting — the same workshops referenced on our Fall Workshops page.
