WiSE

32+ Events
350+ Attendees
Grades 3–8
Since December 2018

Women in STEM Empowerment (WiSE) events are STEM events that aim to empower students in grades 3–8. Each event is staffed by female 1678 volunteers that engage students in fun, hands-on activities and inspire them to consider STEM as a career path.


Since December of 2018, we have held 32 WiSE events, reaching over 350 attendees, receiving amazing responses from parents and the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend?

The WiSE events are intended for girls and nonbinary students in grades 3 through 8, but all students are welcome!

How long is each event?

Each event is approximately one hour and 30 minutes long, and takes place at the Mary L. Stephens Library in the Blanchard Room. Female and nonbinary counselors from Citrus Circuits lead the events at stations.

Is there a cost?

All of the WiSE events are free of charge. Please register through the interest form so we can gauge attendance and adequately prepare.

What materials do I need?

You don't need to bring any materials — just a ready mind to do some fun STEM activities!
