Outreach

Part of our job as an FRC team is to reach out to our community and spread the message of FIRST®. We export our expertise through starting and mentoring DYR and FRC teams, hosting our Diversity In STEM events, holding workshops at our off-season competition Capital City Classic, maintaining a booth at our town's famous Farmers Market, and helping people experiencing homelessness find shelters. We make it a priority during team meetings to always set a time for outreach and to follow through with our ambitious goals.
