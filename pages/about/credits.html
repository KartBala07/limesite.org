A note from the builder

Who Made This

You found the secret page. Since you searched for it, you've earned the real answer: my name is Kartikeya Bala, Class of 2028, and I rebuilt this site myself, for our team, because I love this team.

Our old site had served us well for years, but it was starting to feel its age — static, hard to update, and not really capturing what it's like to be part of Citrus Circuits today. I wanted something that felt as sharp and deliberate as the robots we build: fast, clean, and built with the same care we put into every subsystem in the shop. So I sat down and rebuilt it from the ground up, page by page, picture by picture.

Who I am

I'm Kartikeya Bala, a junior at Davis Senior High School and a proud member of FRC Team 1678, Citrus Circuits. I'm graduating with the Class of 2028, and I designed and built this new website in 2026.

Why I built it this way

Every part of this redesign — the layout, the navigation, the way the pictures are organized, even small details like the theme toggle — was a chance to make this team's story easier to tell and easier to find. I wanted alumni, mentors, sponsors, and future members to land on this site and immediately feel the same pride I feel walking into our shop.

Credit where it's due

I didn't do this completely alone. I worked closely with Claude, an AI assistant from Anthropic, to help write and organize the code, reorganize years of photos into folders that actually make sense, and catch the small mistakes that are easy to miss when you're moving fast. It was a real collaboration — I made the decisions and did the work, and it helped me move faster and build something better than I could have alone in the same amount of time.

Where we came from

None of this replaces our history. If you want to see where this site started, the original Citrus Circuits website is still up:

Visit the original citruscircuits.org →

I built this because Citrus Circuits has given me so much — friendships, late nights in the shop, the feeling of a robot finally working right before a match. This site is my way of giving something back: a home for this team that's built with as much care as everything else we make.

Made with love, for 1678, by Kartikeya Bala — Class of 2028, Davis Senior High School, 2026. Someone who isn't done yet.
