Team 1857 Citrus Sparks

Our Everybot Team

Founded in 2026, Citrus Sparks is our new member Everybot Project team. Freshmen and sophomore team members on our design, electrical, and software robot subteams have the opportunity to build, modify, and compete with Team 118 The Robonauts' Everybot, allowing them to gain sufficient technical and strategic experience during their first year in a competitive environment. The team is led by seven project managers and consists of junior high and high school students from the Davis Joint Unified School District.

The Everybot is a low-cost comprehensive set of build resources to field a competitive and affordable robot intended to play in the FIRST Robotics Competition.
